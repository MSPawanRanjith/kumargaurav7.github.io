Mini Project Exhibition

The first and second year students are the ones who are most enthusiastic about all the clubs an engineering college has to offer, one such club being IEEE. To ensure the active participation of the first as well as second year students, we held a mini project exhibition with the first two year students as participants and the third and final year students as mentors/guide for their project. The exhibition involved groups each consisting of 3-4 members. It was witnessed by Dr. Sriraam sir and other professors.

The projects were exhibited on LHC, APEX and ESB blocks. The event was inaugurated at 11:00 am. Some of the participants came up with their own ideas and others came up with projects as suggested. Projects included

  • Persistence of vision
  • Dice roller
  • Heat sensing robot
  • Edge follower robot
  • Dancing LEDs
  • DTMF robot
  • Water-level controller
  • Ultrasonic distance measurement
  • Mobile phone detector
  • Laser security
  • Tilt detector
  • Gesture based robot
  • Obstacle avoider
  • Infrared proximity detector
    • B
  • uzzer game
  • LED cube
  • Stethoscope
  • Vacuum cleaner
  • Ping Pong game
  • Touch controller

The event was successful with a large number of participants as well as spectators.
