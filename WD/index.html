Web Development Class

MSRIT IEEE offered its students a three-month Web Development class to enhance their skills inÂ HTML 5, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript and a part of php. Lack of time prevented further subjects from being covered. Members of IEEE had the added advantage of attending without a fee while others were allowed to join at a nominal rate of Rs 200.

The mentor of the program, a final year MSRIT Information Science student Mudit Saxena handled his tasks with dedication and capability. With three classes of about two hours conducted weekly the sessions were interesting and informative. There were about 20 students who attended on a regular basis with one of them, Rajni K Sah even going on to design her own website solvemath.in on which the Aptitude Test 2016 was conducted.
